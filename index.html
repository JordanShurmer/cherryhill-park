About

Cherryhill Park Pool is a local community pool in Knoxville, TN. Open to members from the Cherryhill Park neighborhood in Knoxville, TN and other nearby neighborhoods. Fully managed and funded by the members of the community.

The pool includes

  • diving board
  • kiddie pool
  • restrooms
  • an open grill
  • covered picnic tables

Hours

Cherryhill Park Pool is open to members during the Summmer season, while the sun is up.

The pool is only open to members and their guests.
